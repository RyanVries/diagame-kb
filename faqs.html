Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Qs)

The content covered in this document is supposed to be a knowledge base for all DiaGame project members, and also participants of the experiments conducted by the project.

Here the Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Qs) regarding the project and its experiment(s) setup are answered with the intention of providing a simple, intuitive and clean way to understand general matters, and also solve possible issues.

Experiments setup

General

Participants must use a total of three (3) devices during the period stipulated:

Continuous Glucose Monitoring sensor FreestyleLibre Smartwatch Samsung Galaxy Active 2 with DiaGameData application installed Smartphone (owned by the participant) with MijnEetMeter installed.

 In case of the participant not having a smartwatch, the web version of MijnEetmeter can also be used from a desktop or notebook. For this, the same credential/account provided must be used.

Continuous Glucose Monitoring sensor FreestyleLibre
The sensor is applied to the arm of the participant and collects blood glucose level information.

Smartwatch Samsung Galaxy Active 2
The watch contains the application that wil collect sensor related data, as well as mood and insulin data self-reported by each participant.

Smartphone (owned by the participant) with MijnEetMeter installed
Any smartphone able to have MijnEetMeter installed can be used.

Smartwatch

The daily steps/functions that all participants must follow/use regarding the smartwatch are as follows:

  1. Charging

    2. Use the included wireless USB charger. This should be done without turning the watch off.
    Tip: Bath and sleep times are good daily moments for charging.

  2. Wrapping

    3. Wrap the watch firmly around your lower arm just above your wrist.

  3. Using the watch keys/buttons

    • Home: go to main watch screen or turn on/off when held.
    • Back: goes to a previous state. When in the DiaGameData application, it moves back to the main screen.

  4. Open the DiaGameData application

    5. From the Home screen, swipe it left until the icon is seen. Use your finger to tap it, and the app will open. After started, the app will keep running continuously even when in the background.

     If the app closes for any reason (battery drained, system reboot), it must be reopened, and the monitoring resumed manually.

    Tip: you can always (re)open the DiaGameData application and double check in the Sensors screen if it is running. If it is not, just click the "play" button.

Smartwatch

Swiping is used for navigating to different screens on DiaGameData application.

After opening the app, the first screen to be shown is the Mood Log. Swiping to one side will lead to Help. To the other side to the rest of the screens in order: Insulin, Sensors, and User Information.

If the Home button is pressed, the clock interface appears, and the app goes to the background -- but is kept running. Same happens when nothing is done, after some seconds the watch fades to a black screen, and the next time the clock interface will appear. Again, the app is kept running in the background.

Smartwatch

Through DiaGameData application's Insulin screen.

The screen allows the participant to inform the type of insulin taken, the amount, and also the time of the dose.

Smartwatch

Through DiaGameData application's Mood screen.

The screen allows participants to inform how they currently feel. This is done by selecting the right value and confirming.

Note: this screen will appear several times during the day (only from 8:00 to 20:00), and a "buzz" will indicate when this happens. We encourage every participant to inform their mood as soon as the screen appears to them.
